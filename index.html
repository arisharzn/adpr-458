ABOUT ME

I’m an undergraduate student studying Advertising & Public Relations and Hospitality, Restaurant & Tourism Management. I’m also a student-athlete, and one of my long-term goals is to open a bakery in a small town like Lincoln.

I’m a pole vaulter from Russia, currently representing the University of Nebraska. I expected this to be a break from freezing Siberian winters—but Nebraska weather can be just as unpredictable. Outside of track, I enjoy watching TV shows, listening to music, and shopping.
